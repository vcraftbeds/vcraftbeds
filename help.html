How we help

Our support page is built to answer the questions customers ask before buying larger-ticket furniture online.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery timing depends on the product and your location. Many orders are arranged within a few working days, while made-to-order items may take longer.

Can I ask about sizes before ordering?

Yes. Send your room dimensions and preferred bed size and we can help guide you toward the best fit.

Do you help after checkout?

Yes. Once your order is placed, you can still contact us for delivery coordination and order support.

Can I order a custom finish?

For custom requests, contact support before ordering so we can confirm what is possible for your chosen product.
